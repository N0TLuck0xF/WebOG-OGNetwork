OGNetwork Rules

  • Keep it real: Share stuff that’s worth the read—cool ideas, solid info, or legit questions.
  • No noise: Skip the spam, ads, or off-topic rants. Stay on point.
  • Play nice: Respect the crew. No flames, no hate—just straight talk.
  • Quality over clout: Postpoints are for value, not drama. Upvote what’s good, downvote what’s weak.
  • Own it: Post what you mean, mean what you post. No sock puppets or shady moves.
  • Links matter: If you’re dropping a URL, make sure it’s legit and adds something.
  • Forum vibes: Sections are for focus—keep posts where they fit.
  • Break it, sit out: Mods can boot you if you can’t hang with the rules.
